## Content Strategy Notes

### Authority Signals Built Into This Map

1. **Cluster-to-ICP Alignment**: Each topic cluster maps to the ICP with the strongest authority signal
   - AI-native org design → ICP-3 (owns team architecture and AI tooling)
   - Workforce architecture → ICP-1 (founder in reactive hiring crisis)
   - Post-funding hiring → ICP-2 (actively scaling past PMF)

2. **Cross-ICP Questions as Differentiation**: 27 shared questions become opportunities to show nuance
   - Seed founder's answer vs. Series B CEO's answer vs. COO's answer to "hire vs. automate?" all live in same cluster
   - Each ICP variant page can link to variants for other ICPs (creates internal link equity)

3. **Buyer Journey Depth**: 6 distinct stages ensure coverage from "didn't know I had this problem" to "how do I succeed long-term"
   - Problem-aware stage has most questions (22) — this is where educational content converts
   - Decision stage has targeted objection-handling content (16 questions)

4. **Semantic Clustering**: Questions are naturally deduplicated
   - "What is workforce architecture?" appears once (pillar page)
   - ICP variants ask "How do I apply workforce architecture in my specific situation?" (cluster pages)
